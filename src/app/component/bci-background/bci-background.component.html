BCI Background Check

Purpose:
BCI checks are often used for employment, volunteer work, licensing, and other situations where an individual's criminal history needs to be reviewed.
How it Works:
The BCI uses fingerprints to search the state's criminal records database, revealing any criminal history within Ohio.
Obtaining a BCI Check:
You can typically obtain a BCI background check through various authorized locations, including school districts, educational service centers, or local law enforcement agencies.
Fingerprinting:
Fingerprints are required to conduct the BCI check.
Processing Time:
BCI checks generally have a processing time of 1-3 business days for fingerprinting done at certain locations.
