What Is a BCI or FBI Background Check?

BCI Background Check
Conducted by the Ohio Bureau of Criminal Investigation, this check reveals state-level criminal history records and is required for many Ohio-based employment and licensing purposes.
FBI Background Check
This check provides a nationwide criminal history report through the Federal Bureau of Investigation, covering offenses in all 50 states. It’s often required for federal licensing, immigration, or roles involving national-level compliance.
Combined BCI & FBI
Some positions require both state and federal checks to ensure complete background screening. SecurePrints can process both in a single appointment.
