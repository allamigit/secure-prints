About SecurePrints

SecurePrints is a trusted provider of comprehensive background check services, specializing in BCI (Ohio Bureau of Criminal Investigation) and FBI WebCheck fingerprinting solutions. We are committed to helping individuals, businesses, and organizations access fast, reliable, and secure background screening to support hiring, licensing, and other verification needs.
Who We Are
At SecurePrints, we combine state-of-the-art digital fingerprinting technology with a customer-first approach. As an officially certified WebCheck provider, we serve a wide range of industries — including education, healthcare, childcare, finance, legal services, and more — ensuring compliance with local and federal background check requirements.
What We Do
  • BCI Background Checks – For employment, licensing, and volunteer purposes within the state of Ohio.
  • FBI WebCheck – Nationwide criminal background checks for federal clearance or out-of-state use.
  • Mobile Fingerprinting Services – On-site solutions for groups and organizations.
  • Fast & Secure Results – Most background checks processed and returned within days.
